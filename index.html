About Me

I am a PhD student at the Institute for Microwave Engineering and Photonics (IHF) at the University of Stuttgart. My research focuses on applying machine learning and deep learning techniques to solve complex problems in computational electromagnetics, particularly in metasurface design and optimization.

I specialize in developing neural network surrogate models for electromagnetic simulations, with expertise in PyTorch, RCWA methods, and multi-GPU training architectures.
